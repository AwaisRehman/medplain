ORIGINAL:
For type 2 diabetes with hypertension, start metformin 500 mg twice daily with meals, titrating to 1000 mg twice daily after 2 weeks if tolerated, and amlodipine 5 mg once daily. Counsel that metformin should be temporarily withheld on days of contrast imaging or acute illness with dehydration to reduce the risk of lactic acidosis. Recheck HbA1c in 3 months with a target below 7 percent, and monitor blood pressure with a goal under 130/80 mmHg. Report muscle pain, unusual fatigue, or difficulty breathing promptly.
