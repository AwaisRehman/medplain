ORIGINAL:
Following acute ST-elevation myocardial infarction with successful primary PCI and drug-eluting stent to the left anterior descending artery, discharge on dual antiplatelet therapy: aspirin 81 mg daily indefinitely and ticagrelor 90 mg twice daily for 12 months, plus atorvastatin 80 mg nightly, metoprolol succinate 50 mg daily, and lisinopril 10 mg daily. Do not discontinue ticagrelor prematurely without cardiology consultation, as early cessation markedly increases stent thrombosis risk. Follow up in 2 weeks and present immediately for recurrent chest pain.
